# Barnes & Noble UI Analysis

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green:** `#3e5d58` (estimated dark green for buttons, icons, and some text).
- **Background:** `#ffffff` (White).
- **Secondary Backgrounds:**
    - Dark Promo: Dark brown/gold (Fourth Wing banner).
    - Footer Background: Dark grey/green? (Pending footer observation).
- **Text:** Dark grey to black for main content.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Serif font (Elegant, likely Poynter or similar). Used for section titles ("Bestsellers", "Our Monthly Picks").
- **Body Text:** Sans-serif (Clean, likely Lato or Arial). Used for book titles, authors, and buttons.
- **Italics:** Section titles are often italicized.

## UI Components
- **Header:**
    - Centered logo.
    - Hamburger menu (Mobile/Responsive toggle) on left.
    - Icons on right (Stores, Wishlist, Account, Cart).
    - Wide search bar below logo with "All" dropdown and search button.
- **Hero Banner:** Large image carousel with "SHOP NOW" or "PRE-ORDER" buttons.
- **Book Carousels:**
    - Section title with "See All" link on right.
    - Slick carousel with arrows on edges.
    - Book cards: Image, Title (centered), Author (centered), Star Ratings, "QUICK ADD" button (primary green/blue border?).
- **Grid Promos:** 3-column grid for featured categories.
- **Banner Tiles:** Varied background colors (Red/Salmon, Teal) with text and CTA.
